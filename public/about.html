ABOUT

SPENT1

Graffiti artist & Creative designer

“Spent1” was born in 1993 in Heraklion, Crete, Greece. He started as a graffiti writer in 2007.

He studied Materials Science at the University of Crete, Graphic Design at Vellios School of Art (scholarship) and MSc in Art & Design at AKTO and Middlesex University (scholarship).

His work has been presented in exhibitions, commissions, and arts festivals. His works are mainly based on creating abstract compositions with characters combined with geometric patterns and bright colors. His compositions are characterized by multiplicity - alternating colors and techniques. Through his works he wants to communicate his aesthetics and perspective to the audience while letting the observer process the work in his own way.

As part of this activity, "Spent1" has collaborated and worked with world known brands, business groups, hotel groups, cultural organizations, advertising, creative agencies, various artists, etc.

ABOUT

SPENT1

Graffiti artist & Creative designer

The Artist

"Spent1" was born in 1993 in Heraklion, Crete. He started as a graffiti writer in 2007.

He studied Materials Science at the University of Crete, Graphic Design at the Vellideion School of Fine Arts (scholarship) and MSc in Art & Design at AKTO and Middlesex University (scholarship). He is a member of the Greek Chamber of Fine Arts.

His work has been presented in exhibitions, commissions and art festivals. His works are mainly based on creating abstract compositions with characters combined with geometric patterns and bright colours. His compositions are characterised by multiplicity - alternating colours and techniques. Through his works he wants to communicate his aesthetics and perspective to the audience, while allowing the observer to process the work in his own way.

As part of this activity, "Spent1" has collaborated and worked with world renowned brands, business groups, hotel groups, cultural organizations, advertising agencies, creative agencies, various artists, etc.

The Operation

As part of this work, "Spent1" collaborates with a team of qualified professionals to offer integrated design solutions that cover all the client's requirements, such as manufacturing applications, printing solutions, graphic design, etc. In this way offering the client comprehensive solutions of high quality design solutions.
